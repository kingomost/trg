Silver All Inclusive Package	  
For travellers of all ages. Travellers age 75 and older must complete a medical questionnaire to get a quote and purchase this policy. This comprehensive coverage was designed with the budget conscious traveller in mind. A few of the benefits included in the package: Trip Cancellation (100% of trip cost), Trip Interruption, Baggage and Personal Effects; Emergency Medical Expense ($2 Million); and many more.
